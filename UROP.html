Summer 2020 Undergraduate Research Opportunties

There are several opportunities for undergraduate research placements in my group focusing on exoplanets, planet formation and accretion discs. These placements will be funded (between Â£1000-2000, depending on available funding and length of the placement) and the student will participate in weekly group meetings and seminars. To be considered, you must be able to undertake paid undergraduate work in the UK and be at the end of your 2nd year or higher of studies by the time you start. Placements are for a minimum of 8 weeks (shorter will not be considered) and will take place between the end of June and end of September.

In order to apply, please send a Cover Letter describing your interests and availability, and a CV with module grades as a single PDF to owen.exoplanet.group !**at**! gmail.com. The deadline to receive applications is 15th March 2020, and successful applicants will be contacted shortly afterwards.
